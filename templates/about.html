About Us

Empowering individuals with early disease detection and insights.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable and accessible platform for individuals to identify potential health issues early. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, we aim to contribute to healthier communities through early detection and prevention.

What We Offer

  • Accurate Disease Prediction: Analyze symptoms using AI to provide probable diagnoses.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for easy interaction.
  • Comprehensive Symptom Database: Covers a wide range of diseases and symptoms.
  • Secure Patient Data: Ensures confidentiality and privacy for all users.

How It Works

The Disease Prediction System uses a combination of supervised machine learning models, including Decision Trees. Users can input their symptoms into the system, and it processes this data to predict potential health issues. The system provides recommendations to consult medical professionals for further diagnosis and treatment.

Feature Process of the Project

The system follows a step-by-step process to provide accurate disease predictions:

  • Step 1: User Login/Registration - Users must log in or register to access the system. This ensures data security and personalized predictions.
  • Step 2: Patient Form - Users fill out a form with their basic details (e.g., name, age, gender) to help the system tailor predictions.
  • Step 3: Symptom Selection - Users select symptoms from a predefined list or enter them manually.
  • Step 4: Disease Prediction - The system processes the symptoms using machine learning algorithms (e.g., Decision Tree) to predict potential diseases.
  • Step 5: Result Display - The predicted disease(s) are displayed along with recommendations for further action.
  • Step 6: Patient History - Users can view their past predictions and track their health over time.

Technology Stack

Our system is built with the following technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (Flask)
  • Database: Firebase
  • Machine Learning Models: Decision Tree

Why Choose Us?

In a world where timely medical intervention can save lives, our platform serves as a first step in understanding health conditions. We strive to empower individuals by providing them with early insights into their health, promoting awareness, and enabling proactive care.
